Common First Questions

How fast can you respond to an emergency HVAC situation?
Jason prioritizes same-day or next-morning response for systems that are completely down, especially in extreme weather. Call or text directly at 518.209.9636.
Do you work with any contractors you'd recommend?
Yes — Jason has a network of vetted local contractors. He'll never require you to use them, but he knows who does quality work at fair prices in the Capital Region.
Can you review a proposal I already have in hand?
Absolutely. A proposal review is one of the most common one-time requests. Send it over before you sign — that's exactly when it matters most.
