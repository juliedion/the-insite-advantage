The Tier 2 Scope of Work is specifically designed to make all quotes comparable. The moment Jason is also in the installation business, his advice about which system to buy and which contractor to hire becomes compromised. A second contractor opinion is still a sales call. The contractor giving you that opinion wants your business — which means they have financial incentives to tell you the first quote was wrong in ways that favor their own proposal. Jason is not selling the equipment, not performing the installation, and not receiving any compensation from whoever eventually does. His only financial relationship is with you, which means his analysis of competing proposals is the one analysis in the room with no stake in the outcome. That is a structural difference, not a degree difference.
